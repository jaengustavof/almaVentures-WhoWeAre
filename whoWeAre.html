OUR FOUNDERS

MARK HUNTER

"We are what our thoughts have made us; so take care about what you think. Words are secondary. Thoughts live; they travel far"

Angel investor in Alma Ventures, as well as in other 78 start-ups. Based in St. Petersburg, Florida, he is also a pilot, former monk, and co-founder of private equity fund KLH Capital. Hunter has unified his eclectic interest in mysticism, effective altruism, minimalism, and entrepreneurship in an ingenious holistic approach to doing business: boosting social potential while generating circular profit in pivotal neighborhoods in the US.
